## My Tasks

### Task 1: Spreading Digital Awareness
I created an educational infographic to help others understand critical digital topics like "Identifying Fake News" or "Safe Social Media Habits." I used tools like **Canva** to design this visual guide.

### Task 2: Establishing My Professional Presence
I have set up and optimized my professional profiles on **LinkedIn**, **GitHub**, and **Kaggle**. These platforms allow me to showcase my technical skills, network with professionals, and host my future projects.

### Task 3: Strengthening My Logic
To demonstrate my computational thinking, I solved beginner-level logic challenges on **HackerRank**. I have documented my progress and successful submissions through screenshots included in my report.

### Task 4: Practicing Professional Etiquette
I drafted professional communications for two real-world scenarios: requesting an internship and asking for a deadline extension. This task helped me refine my digital "netiquette" and formal writing skills.

### Task 5: Prioritizing Cyber Security
I developed a personal Cyber-Safety Checklist and analyzed a case study of a recent cyber-fraud incident. This ensures I am equipped to protect my digital identity and financial information.
