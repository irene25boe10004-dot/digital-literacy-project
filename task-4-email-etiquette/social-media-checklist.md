Task 4: Part B: Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

&#x20;

Do's:

* Lock down privacy settings to control who sees your history
* Build a professional brand by sharing projects and wins.
* Fact-check info before you spread it to your network.
* Network with alumni to find internship opportunities.
* Google yourself to see what recruiters will find.



Don'ts:

* Post while emotional or under the influence.
* Share "live" locations or private data like your dorm info.
* Badmouth professors or peers in a public forum.
* Engage in toxic arguments or "rage-bait" comments.
* Let scrolling cut into your sleep or study time.
