**Task 5: Part B**

'Stay Safe Online Checklist'

1. UPI Caution: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money or "refunds."
2. Verify Recipients: Check the actual name on your UPI app before confirming any payment.
3. Secure Accounts: Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on WhatsApp, Instagram, and college emails.
4. Public Wi-Fi: Use a VPN when accessing student portals or banking on campus/hostel Wi-Fi.
5. App Hygiene: Deny unnecessary permissions (camera, contacts) for "free" study or photo apps.
6. Spot Fakes: Avoid clicking "KYC Update" or "Account Blocked" links sent via SMS/WhatsApp.
7. Official Sites: Always check for https:// and valid domains (like .ac.in or .gov.in) for exam forms.
8. Card Safety: Do not save debit/credit card details on food delivery or shopping apps.







If you face cyber fraud or harassment, act immediately:



Helpline: Dial 1930 (24/7 National Cyber Crime Helpline)



Portal: Report at cybercrime.gov.in
